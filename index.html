WHAT I DO

DESIGN

UI/UX is considered the most important part of any website these days. Like every development phase should start I also like to start by designing the different UIs and UXs for the websites I build and I always try to make my designs responsive. My design process is very simple. I start out by sketching on paper then take that into photoshop and transform it into the different mockups using different style guidlines.

DEVELOPMENT

After the design phase, I take the diffenet mockups and implement them using diffenent web technologies available at hand. My development process consists of two subphases, Frontend and Backend, depending on client requirements. I always try to use the standard implemnetation algorithms when needed.
